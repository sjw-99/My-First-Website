A Brief History of Sam Wood

1999 - Swindon

I grew up in Swindon, a railway town in Wiltshire. Swindon gets a lot of (quite rightly) bad press, but it is also home to the mighty Swindon Town Football Club and there are plenty of green spaces around the edges to escape to.

2022 - Oxford

Having graduated from my Masters, I then studied for a PGCE in Science at St. Anne's College, University of Oxford. My first placement school was full of wonderful students and staff, and so I stayed for three years teaching Maths & Science.

2018 - Birmingham

I moved to Birmingham to study Theoretical Physics, and although my time there was disrupted by the pandemic, I fell in love with the city! My degree was fascinating, and I still enjoy some recreational Maths & Physics problems to this day.

2025 - Porto

This year I took the decision to switch careers and pursue new opportunities in tech. In the time since the summer term ended I discovered my new favourite place in the the world, Porto, whilst travelling with my partner.
